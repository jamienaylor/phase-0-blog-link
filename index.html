Blog Link: Phase 0 Portfolio Project

1) What went well? Key Learning.

The thing i found in which i suprised myself with was the ability to adapt to and learn the basic git elements, commands and language. This is something i enjoyed as i found it extremely rewarding.

I also enjoyed the HTML labs, and i found that the biggest and best tool for me to use was Google.

2) What could have been improved?

An area i have somewhat lagged in is the CSS language, and referncing that back to the HTML file. I have a lot to learn in this area still before i feel somewhat confident. The varying class references were probably my biggest challenge, however with the assitance of Google i did manage to write some ok code for my web page.

3) The key take aways.

The key take away for me through this first phase of training was the fact that i was still able to succeed on labs even though i may not have known how to complete them without in depth assitance from Google. I still feel like im a long way from a confidence level of which ill be happy with, however i dont doubt i will eventually make it to a position im happy with.

4) The process you followed?

The process i followed and found easiest was to ensure i took each lab step by step, and often as i progressed onto another lab i would keep the previous one open as i knew the new lab would be building off the previous one.

I managed to find myself in a routine for opening repositories that worked well for me, and i just tried to mimic the data and content that was provided within the labs so that i had an easy and direct reference for things when ever i got stuck. If i had of changed content i dont think i would have been able to come to terms with understanding the labs as quickly as i did.
